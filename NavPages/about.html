The Owners

What started out as a conversation between two strangers overtime blossomed into a beautiful partnership between best friends. ReJuicin8 Juice Bar was created by Shonta Hazel and Diandra S. Sabb.

The vision was to start an “exclusive” juice bar in Downtown Savannah, Georgia. They wanted a juice bar where customers can come to unwind, relax, and enjoy healthy food items and cold-pressed juices. With this vision in mind, Diandra and Shonta began experimenting with various recipes until they found the right combinations for each juice. As their passion for juicing began to grow each day, so did their drive to start a juice bar. With God at the forefront, their dream became a reality as they opened their first location in 2023. Both ladies are no stranger to sacrifice and hard work. Shonta is a former Mental Health Counselor with a master’s degree in Social Work, and Diandra is a former Police Sergeant with a master’s degree in Business Administration.
